What we offer

This course provides a comprehensive way to start your computer science journey. Throughout the 3-5 years you will develop a basic understanding of how computers work along with an intro to python and C++. These courses also offer opportunities to recieve cerifications in python and C++. Upon completion of these courses will be elegable to recieve an Associates in Computer Science. See https://ict.gctaa.net/ for details about individual courses.


Our recommened path

Our computer programming course is a three-year sequence of Dual Enrolled classes, focusing on basic understanding of how computers work.
Students who complete all three years (as well as additional general education requirements) are eligible to receive an Associates in Computer Science.
