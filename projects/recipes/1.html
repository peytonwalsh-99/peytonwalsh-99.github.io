Pancakes

"They make good frisbees." -My twin (I'm the evil one)

Ingrediants

  • 1 egg
  • 1 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp whit sugar
  • 3 tbsp vegitable oil
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • A dash of salt
  1. Mix all ingrediants until batter is smooth. If it is too thick, add more milk. If it is too thin, add more flour.
  2. Heat up a medium pan on medium heat.
  3. Grease the pan with some butter or cooking oil to prevent sticking.
  4. To cook the pancakes, pour aproximently 1/4 cup of batter on the pan per pancake. You should be able to make 2-3 at a time.

    5. Congrats! You have pancakes.
