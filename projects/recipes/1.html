Tips and substitutes

You can use any kind of milk, dairy or non-dairy and it will stay the same.
If you don't have vegitable oil, you can use the same amount of melted butter.
If you don't have white sugar, brown sugar works fines.
Whole wheat flower can also be used instead of all-purpose, but only use 1 1/4 cups.

Ingrediants

Ingrediant Amount
Egg 1
Whole Milk 1 1/2 cups
White sugar 2 tbsp
Vegitable oil 3 tbsp
All purpose flour 1 1/2 cups
Baking powder 3 1/2 tsp
Vanilla A dash
  1. Mix all ingrediants until batter is smooth. If it is too thick, add more milk. If it is too thin, add more flour.
  2. Heat up a medium pan on medium heat.
  3. Grease the pan with some butter or cooking oil to prevent sticking.
  4. To cook the pancakes, pour aproximently 1/4 cup of batter on the pan per pancake. You should be able to make 2-3 at a time. Flip after 30 seconds, let the other side cook for the same amount of time.

Congrats. You have pancakes.

Prep time: 5 minutes, Cook time:1 minute per pancake
Probably makes 10-12 medium pancakes
